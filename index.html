Privacy Policy for Ordly

Your privacy is important to us! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when using Ordly.

1. Data Collection

To enable account creation and save your progress, we collect and store the following information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Encrypted password
  • Game statistics and progress

This data is used solely to provide a personalized experience and track your achievements in the game.

2. Inactive Accounts

Accounts that remain inactive for more than one year will be deleted from our system.

3. Account Deletion

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please do so using this link.

4. Crash Analytics

To improve app performance and stability, we collect anonymous crash analytics data. This data helps us identify and fix issues in the app. No personal information is collected through crash analytics.

5. How We Use Collected Data

The data we collect is used only to:

  • Enable account login and save game progress.
  • Ensure a smooth and enjoyable gaming experience.
  • Improve app performance based on general usage data.
  • Resolve app crashes and enhance stability.

We do not sell, share, or otherwise make your data available to third parties.

6. Data Protection

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect the data stored on our servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Children and User Data

Ordly is not specifically designed for children under 13, but no sensitive personal data is collected. If we become aware of any personal data collected from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be published here, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
